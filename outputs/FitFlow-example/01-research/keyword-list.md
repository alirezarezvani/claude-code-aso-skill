- Primary keywords: running, cycling, GPS tracker
- **Gap:** Not targeting beginners or casual users
- **Opportunity:** Emphasize "easy" and "beginner-friendly"

**Nike Run Club (4.7★, 300k+ ratings):**
- Primary keywords: running, audio coaching, training plans
- **Gap:** Running-focused only, less general fitness
- **Opportunity:** Broader "fitness" and "workout" focus

**MapMyRun (4.5★, 200k+ ratings):**
- Primary keywords: run tracker, route mapping
- **Gap:** Technical, serious runners
- **Opportunity:** Simpler UI, casual audience

**MyFitnessPal (4.7★, 600k+ ratings):**
- Primary keywords: calorie counter, nutrition, diet
- **Gap:** Nutrition-focused, less workout tracking
- **Opportunity:** Focus on workout planning and activity tracking

**Fitbit (4.6★, 400k+ ratings):**
- Primary keywords: fitness tracker, steps, heart rate
- **Gap:** Requires Fitbit device
- **Opportunity:** Standalone app, no device required
